What is Origins?

Origins is a Minecraft mod for Fabric that allows you to choose an Origin at the beginning of the game.
There are a variety of Origins to choose from, each with its own benefits and drawbacks.

You'll be greeted with the origin select screen the first time you join the game.
Each origin has something new to discover, so come and find something new at Unnamed!
